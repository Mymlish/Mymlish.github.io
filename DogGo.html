DogGo

One of the courses here at KTH was Media Technology and Interaction Design (DM2609). In this course we learned about different methods in all stages of a design process, which was applied to a project.

Through this project DogGo was born. A mobile app designed for petowners, where they can connect with other petowners, plan hangouts for their pets, and let other petowners know whether they have time for a playdate or not.
